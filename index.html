About us:

Our environment encompasses everything that surrounds us, including both living (biotic) and non-living (abiotic) components. It's the sum of all factors that influence an organism's survival and includes elements like air, water, soil, climate, plants, and animals. The environment is crucial for life on Earth, providing resources and shaping interactions between living things and their surroundings.
